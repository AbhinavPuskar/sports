The equipments used in Cricket are as follows

(1) Bat-A red white or pink ball wuth a cork base,wrapped in twine covered with leather.The ball should have a circumference of 23 cm in international matches
(2)Ball- A wooden bat is used .The wood used is from the Kashmir or English willow tree.The bat cannot be more than 38 inches (96.5 cm) long and 4.25 inches(10.8) wide.Aluminium bat is not allowed
(3)Stumps-Three upright wooden poles that ,together with the bails,from the wicket.
(4)Bails-Two  crosspeices made of wood,placed on top of stumps.
(5)Sight Screen-A screen placed  at the boundary
